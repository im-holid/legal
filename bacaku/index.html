Privacy Policy for Bacaku

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to "Bacaku," an educational app designed for children aged 3–6. Your privacy and your child's safety are our top priorities. This app is designed to be fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2. Information Collection

The app is used anonymously. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. We may collect non-personal technical data (e.g., device model, OS version, and crash logs) solely to ensure the app works correctly on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services for app functionality and internal operations:

  • Google AdMob: We show family-safe ads. AdMob may use anonymous identifiers for frequency capping and limited reporting.
  • RevenueCat: Processes anonymous transaction data for In-App Purchases (Premium). No personal identity is stored.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Used to monitor app performance and fix bugs. We have disabled "Advertising ID" collection to protect child privacy.
  • Firebase Remote Config: Used to manage app updates and configurations.
  • Speech Recognition: Uses the device's native API. Voice data is processed locally and is NOT recorded or stored by us.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our data collection is limited to "internal operations" as permitted by COPPA to support the app's functions.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at: dawp.animation@gmail.com
